# EDR-KE-011: Engineering Assertion Promotion

Status: Deferred

Target:
Assertion and promotion workflow slice

Decision:
Engineering assertions are recognized as first-class conceptual project propositions, but their authoritative promotion workflow is deferred until the repository defines assertion identity, lifecycle, and review semantics explicitly.

Required future clarification:

- assertion identity and versioning
- assertion supersession and retraction
- assertion verification workflow
- promotion of parsed or AI-proposed assertions
- interaction between assertions, observations, and requirements
